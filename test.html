ONE%

“No one has ever become poor by giving”

― Anne Frank

Why ONE%?

If everyone who has more than enough to live comfortably would donate just ONE% of their savings to people that really need it. People who lack food, clean water, or just a shelter to spend the night. Our main objective is to show that you can donate just ONE% of your income and redirect your donation to three non-profit organizations working in Africa.

Donation Benefits

The Three H's

Hope

Having hope means that we are waiting for better things to come and we are always expecting a better tomorrow. By donating you can bring hope for those who can be desperate and are in need for basic supplies. You can reach out and show them there is always someone who cares without them even knowing!

Happiness

A small act of kindness can bring a smile to a child's face and change their day. Your donation can bring happiness to many kids in many different communities. By helping the kids you are also bringing joy and happiness to the entire community and the whole environment around!

Humanity

Humanity can have so many different meanings and definitions, but we believe that the most important one is about having compassion and helping one another. If we would care for one another just as we care for our closest relatives and friends the world would definitly be a better place for everyone!
