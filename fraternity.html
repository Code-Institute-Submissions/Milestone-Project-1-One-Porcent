Fraternity Without Borders!


Fraternity Without Borders is a UK registered charity caring for one of the poorest regions of the world: Mozambique. Their main target is to assist orphaned and/or vulnerable children, many with HIV. Since 2009 they have been providing clear water, food, education, cultural activities and basic healthcare to more than 9000 vulnerable children and vulnerable elderly people in 30 community centres

In Mozambique 54% of the population lives below the national poverty line more than 70% of poor households live in rural areas, 63% of rural children live in absolute poverty and it is estimated that over 2 million orphans live in Mozambique, and up to a quarter of the total is due to HIV/AIDS.

Fraternity without borders are working to provide clean water, to improve malnutrition, to improve healthcare, promotion cultural sustainability and also giving free vocational courses.
