Make a child smile today!


A smile is a powerful gesture, every smile has it own uniqueness. It is also contagious and it can improve anyone's mood. Different from most of the adults most of the times kids smile for small and simple things. And this is the exact point of why you can donate any amount and still make such a difference on a kid's life!

Happiness is only real when shared!


This phrase written by Christopher McCandless has such a huge and important meaning behind it. It is sad that he had to lose everything behind and including his life before realizing this. But he leaves such a great lesson behind, and the happiness we can feel while donating and making a difference in someone's life is explained by this famous quote.

Contribute to a better world!


Like the song "Imagine" from John Lennon says: Imagine all the people,Sharing all the world". If everyone could share just a small portion of their income they would contribute so much to create a better world, a world without poverty, without misery, without starvation. As human beings we need to have faith that one day we will be able to achieve this.

Show how grateful you are!


Sometimes with our day-to-day busy routine we forget how luck and privileged we are for having a safe house, food and being able to live without worrying about our next meal. In fact, if we have food in our fridge, clothes on our back, a roof over ours heads and a place to sleep we are richer than 75% of the world. And this is a huge thing to be grateful for.
