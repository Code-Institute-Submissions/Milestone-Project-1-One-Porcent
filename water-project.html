The Water Project!


The water project is a charity and non-profit organization that provides access to clean, safe and reliable water and sanitation solutions across Africa. They invest in local soluctions to the local water crisis. Providing access to clean, safe water helps capable and determined people realize the hope they have in their own futures.

Children often bear the burden of walking miles each day to find water in streams and ponds, full of water-borne disease that is making them and their families sick and the time lost fetching it robs entire communities of their futures.

Girls under the age of 15 are twice as likely as boys to be the family member responsible for fetching water. We believe she deserves to take water for granted, just like you. To pursue a hopeful future, she needs water - every day.
